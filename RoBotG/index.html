About Robo Genius

The Robo Genius Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Global Challenge is an international robotics and artificial intelligence event system. Robo Genius covers robotics, artificial intelligence, online programming, and other categories, meeting the advanced development needs of students.

Vietnam Robo Genius

The Vietnam Robo Genius National Championship 2024 will be hosted by IPPG to select and sponsor teams representing Vietnam to participate in the international Robo Genius Global Challenge using advanced humanoid AI robots from UBTECH gathering 10,000+ participants from countries around the world.
